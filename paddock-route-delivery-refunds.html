01Where we deliver

At launch, we deliver across the Southern Downs and Granite Belt regions of Queensland. Our current zones include:

  • Warwick and surrounds (postcode 4370)
  • Stanthorpe and surrounds (postcode 4380)
  • Allora, Killarney, Yangan, Pratten and other Southern Downs townships
  • Selected Granite Belt townships

If you're unsure whether we deliver to your address, enter your postcode at checkout or get in touch and we'll let you know.

Going further afield
We're expanding regionally and nationally. Join the waitlist on our home page to be notified when we open up your area.

02Delivery schedule

Boxes are delivered weekly on the day allocated to your delivery zone. Your delivery day will be confirmed when you sign up and is shown in your account dashboard.

If a public holiday or unexpected disruption affects your delivery day, we'll email you to confirm the revised schedule for that week.

03Delivery day and times

Deliveries typically run between 8:00am and 6:00pm on your scheduled day. We'll send you a notification with a narrower window (usually 2–3 hours) on the morning of delivery, and a second notification when your driver is on the way.

We can't guarantee a specific time — runs are sequenced to keep produce cold and to keep our drivers' days manageable — but you can leave delivery instructions in your account.

04Being home for delivery

You don't need to be home for your delivery. If you've authorised an unattended delivery, your driver will:

  • leave the Box in the location you nominate (front porch, back gate, carport, etc.);
  • place it out of direct sun where possible; and
  • take a photo of the delivered Box for your records.
Heat and shade
Boxes contain a chilled liner and frozen gel packs and are designed to keep produce cool for up to 4 hours in summer conditions. Even so, please bring your Box inside as soon as possible after delivery — risk in the goods passes to you on delivery.

05Failed or missed deliveries

If we can't deliver your Box because:

  • nobody is home and you haven't authorised unattended delivery;
  • access to the address is blocked or unsafe; or
  • the address details you provided are incorrect or incomplete,

your driver will attempt to contact you and, if needed, return the Box to our depot. We'll get in touch to arrange next steps.

Where possible, we'll redeliver the same day or the next business day. Because produce is perishable, redelivery may not always be possible — in which case we'll work with you on a credit or replacement Box.

06Damaged or missing items

If your Box arrives damaged, or an item is missing or spoiled on arrival, please let us know within 48 hours of delivery with:

  • your order number;
  • a brief description of the issue; and
  • a photo of the damaged or missing item if possible.

The fastest way is to email info@paddockroute.com.au or message us on Instagram or Facebook. We'll respond within one business day and offer one of:

  • a replacement item in your next Box;
  • a credit to your account; or
  • a refund to your original payment method.

07Quality issues with produce

We work directly with growers and pack-to-order to keep your Box as fresh as possible. Even so, fresh produce is variable and occasionally an item won't be up to scratch.

If you're not happy with the quality of an item, please let us know within 48 hours of delivery. The same options apply as for damaged or missing items: replacement, credit, or refund.

We may ask you to share a photo so we can give feedback to the grower — it helps us close the loop quickly.

08Substitutions

Because we source seasonally and weather can disrupt supply, we may occasionally need to substitute an item in your Box. When we do, we'll:

  • substitute with an item of equal or greater value;
  • try to match the type of item (a leafy green for a leafy green, a stone fruit for a stone fruit); and
  • note the substitution in your delivery email or on the packing slip.

If you've told us about an allergy or strong dislike, we'll do our best to avoid it. Please flag allergies clearly in your account so the whole team can see them.

09Refunds and credits

Refunds and credits are processed as follows:

  • Account credits appear in your dashboard immediately and are applied to your next order automatically.
  • Refunds to your original payment method are processed within 3 business days. Your bank or card issuer may take a further 2–10 business days to clear the refund to your account.

We don't generally offer refunds for orders cancelled after the cutoff time (see section 11) because your Box has already been packed or is being prepared. Your rights under the Australian Consumer Law are not affected.

10Pause, skip and cancel

Your subscription is flexible. From your account dashboard you can:

  • Skip a single week's delivery;
  • Pause for a defined period (great for holidays); or
  • Cancel your subscription at any time, with no exit fee.

All changes must be made before the cutoff time for the next delivery to take effect that week.

11Order changes and cutoff times

To make sure we order the right quantities from our growers, our weekly cutoff is:

Weekly cutoff
11:59pm Sunday for the following week's delivery (Monday–Saturday).

Any changes made before the cutoff — skips, pauses, swaps, address changes — will apply to the next delivery. Changes made after the cutoff will apply to the following week.

12Your consumer guarantees

Australian Consumer Law
Our goods come with guarantees that cannot be excluded under the Australian Consumer Law. You are entitled to a replacement or refund for a major failure and to compensation for any other reasonably foreseeable loss or damage. You are also entitled to have the goods repaired or replaced if the goods fail to be of acceptable quality and the failure does not amount to a major failure.

Nothing in this policy limits any rights you have under the Australian Consumer Law or any other law that cannot be excluded.
