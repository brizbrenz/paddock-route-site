The process

Simple as a Sunday morning. ☕

1 🌱

Choose your box

Pick the size that suits your household. Contents rotate with the seasons — because that's what actually grows.

2 🚜

Farmers harvest

Your order is picked across Warwick, Stanthorpe, and the Granite Belt — within 48 hours of your delivery.

3 📦

We pack it

Packed by hand with a farm card naming every grower and simple recipe ideas for anything unfamiliar.

4 🚗

A local delivers it

Delivered by someone in your community. Skip, pause, or cancel anytime — no lock-in, ever.

Drive with Paddock Route

Run your own
local route.

Paddock Route drivers are independent operators who run weekly delivery routes in their area. When your region reaches enough subscribers and grower partners, you go live. No boss, no targets — just a reliable weekly route with your community.

📍
Your patch, your schedule
You set the route and delivery days. We handle subscriptions, orders, and payments.

Boxes are packed and ready for you to collect. No sourcing, no customer service, no hassle.

Our promise

Why it matters.

You know your farmer

Every box names the farms behind your produce. No anonymous supply chains.

Genuinely seasonal

Your box reflects what's actually growing near you right now — not interstate shelf-fillers.

Fair for growers

We pay growers directly and promptly, at fair prices. No auction floors, no retailer margins.

No lock-in

Pause, skip, or cancel with a single tap. No phone calls, no fine print, no catch.

Fresher produce

Paddock to door in 48 hours. Supermarket chains average 7–14 days. The difference is real.

Community-powered

Local growers, local drivers, local customers. The money circulates where it belongs.

Built to stay full

When a bad season hits one region, our grower network can step in from neighbouring areas. You get a full box and a story — not an apology.

Less waste

Picked to order, packed in returnable crates. No shrink-wrap, no shelf-life write-offs, no produce trucked back to a warehouse.
