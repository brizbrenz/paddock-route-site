Drive with us

Driver Program

Run your own local route. Paddock Route drivers are independent operators who deliver weekly Boxes in their own community — no boss, no targets.

The role

Your patch, your route

Paddock Route drivers are independent operators who run weekly delivery routes in their area. When your region reaches enough subscribers and grower partners, you go live. We handle subscriptions, orders and payments — you handle the last mile, on a route you own.

Why drive with us

What the program offers

📍

Your schedule

You set the route and delivery days. We handle subscriptions, orders and payments behind the scenes.

💰

Per-box earnings

Earn a fixed rate per Box delivered. As your region grows, so does your route — and your income.

📦

We do the logistics

Boxes are packed and ready for you to collect. No sourcing, no customer service, no hassle.

🌱

Something real

You're the link between a local farm and a local kitchen. In a small community, that matters.

How it works

From interest to your first run

Register your interest

Tell us where you are and what vehicle you have. It costs you nothing and puts you on the map.

Your region builds up

As growers commit and subscribers join the waitlist, your area moves toward going live.

First right of refusal

When your area is ready to launch, registered drivers get first right of refusal on the route.

Onboard and go live

Quick onboarding, then you start running weekly deliveries on a route that's yours.

What you need

The basics

  • A reliable vehicle — sedan, hatchback, SUV, wagon, van or ute all work
  • A current driver's licence and the right to work in Australia
  • Knowledge of your local area and a few hours one day a week to start
  • An ABN as an independent operator (we can point you in the right direction)
No vehicle yet?

That's fine — register your interest anyway and note it. We'd rather know you're keen and sort logistics later than miss you entirely.
