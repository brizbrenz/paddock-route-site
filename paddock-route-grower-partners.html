For growers

Grower Partners

A committed weekly order, direct payment, and your farm's story told to every household that opens a Box. No middlemen, no market guessing.

The idea

Local food, kept local

Paddock Route connects Southern Downs and Granite Belt growers directly with households in the region — without produce travelling to a Brisbane distribution centre and back. Customers subscribe to a weekly Box, we source as much as possible locally, and growers get a reliable weekly order instead of the uncertainty of markets or retail.

What you get

Why growers partner with us

📦

A committed weekly order

Volumes agreed in advance so you can plan your picking and packing — no surplus guessing.

💰

Direct payment within 7 days

No market fees, no retail markups eating your margin. You deal with the business directly, not a procurement department.

🌾

Your story, told

Every customer who opens a Box sees the farms it came from. Your name travels with your produce.

🤝

A real relationship

We start with a visit to your property to understand what you grow and when. Flexible arrangements that suit your operation.

What we're looking for

Is it a fit?

We're starting with a pilot of around 30 Boxes per week and scaling from there. We partner with a handful of anchor growers across different produce types, so no single farm is overcommitted.

  • Consistent weekly supply of seasonal produce — volumes agreed together
  • Reliable quality that customers will recognise by your farm's name
  • An operation that wants a predictable revenue stream alongside existing channels
  • Fruit, vegetables, eggs, dairy, honey, preserves or quality pantry goods
The marketplace

List direct, sell direct

Beyond the weekly Box, partner growers can list produce and goods on the Paddock Route marketplace. Customers order the exact quantities they want, delivered with their next Box — another channel for your produce with no extra logistics on your side.

How to come on board
